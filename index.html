About Me

I obtained my Master’s degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering. In 2018 I worked as a trainee on Unmanned Aerial Vehicle(UAV) systems in Aalto University (Helsinki,Finland). Right now I work as a Research Assistant in Centre for Research and Technology Hellas (CERTH) in Thessaloniki,Greece as part of the Visual Computing Lab team. I am interested in Deep Learning, Computer Vision and Robotics. Most of my research is about action and image recognition using deep learning techniques.
